# B-roll themes: voiceover + b-roll

Reference list of common b-roll categories used in voiceover+broll construction ads, with example queries that perform well as literal yt-dlp / TikTok / stock-library search strings.

## Rules of thumb

- Concrete actions beat scenery. "hammering shingles" > "roof contractor at work".
- Region in the query when possible. "dallas suburban home drone" > "suburban home drone".
- Vertical framing assumed for 9x16. Either say "vertical" in the query or trust the selector to crop.
- Length: 3-5s clips, occasionally 6-8s for hero shots. Anything longer kills pacing.

## Categories

### Establish (set the scene, intro segment)

- "texas suburban roof drone shot"
- "dallas residential street aerial"
- "single family home exterior daytime"
- "neighborhood home wide vertical"

Intent: locate the viewer in the region. Match the home style to the target ICP (suburban vs. ranch vs. urban).

### Problem (illustrate the pain)

- "missing roof shingles close up"
- "water damage ceiling stain interior"
- "old worn shingles texture"
- "cracked stucco siding house"
- "leak drip bucket ceiling"

Intent: make the cost feel real. Pair with VO that names a dollar figure.

### Cost / consequence

- "money cash counting hands"
- "calculator bill receipt closeup"
- "insurance paperwork denied stamp"
- "contractor estimate handwritten"

Intent: tie the visual to a number the VO just stated.

### Process / solution

- "roof crew installing shingles"
- "construction worker measuring rafter"
- "drone shot new roof complete"
- "kitchen remodel before after side by side"
- "crew installing siding panels"

Intent: show competence. The viewer needs to believe this gets done.

### Social proof / human

- "happy homeowner shaking hands contractor"
- "family in front of new home"
- "owner walking job site"
- "review 5 star rating phone screen"

Intent: trust signal. Keep it real, not stock-photo glossy.

### CTA / outro setup

- "phone screen quote form scrolling"
- "fingers tapping smartphone"
- "calendar booking confirmation"
- "doorbell ring contractor arriving"

Intent: visualize the action the CTA asks for. "Tap below" pairs with phone-tap b-roll.

## Niche-specific shortcuts

### Roofing

- "asphalt shingle installation closeup"
- "hail damage roof texas"
- "roof inspection drone footage"
- "gutter cleaning rain debris"

### Remodeling / interior

- "kitchen countertop quartz install"
- "bathroom remodel before after"
- "open concept living room renovation"
- "hardwood flooring installation crew"

### Exterior / siding / windows

- "vinyl siding house wrap install"
- "window replacement crew working"
- "fence post installation backyard"

## What does NOT work

- Pure abstract concepts ("success", "growth", "trust"). The selector can't score these.
- Glossy stock-photo families looking at the camera. Reads as fake instantly.
- Watermarked clips from competitors' ads. Legal liability + obvious to viewers.
- Slow-mo "cinematic" wide shots with no action. They burn runtime and tell no story.
