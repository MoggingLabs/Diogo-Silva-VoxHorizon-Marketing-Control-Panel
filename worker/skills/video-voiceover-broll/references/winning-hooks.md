# Winning hooks: voiceover + b-roll

A short, opinionated list of hook patterns that have moved the needle on construction lead-gen creatives. Use as starting points, not boilerplate. Customize the region, the offer, and the number.

The point of a hook in a voiceover+broll video: stop the thumb in <3 seconds. Curiosity beats hype. A real number beats an adjective. A pattern interrupt beats a friendly greeting.

## Curiosity

- "Most [target] don't know their [thing] is failing."
- "There's one thing every [target] in [region] gets wrong about [topic]."
- "Here's what I wish I'd known before I [event] my [thing]."
- "If your [thing] looks like this, you've already got a problem."
- "I'll tell you the one mistake that costs [target] [$N] a year."

## Pattern interrupt

- "Stop scrolling if you live in [region]."
- "Don't replace your [thing] until you watch this."
- "[N] [thing] every Texas homeowner needs to see."
- "Skip ahead. This isn't another roofing ad."
- "If you own a home in [region], read this before your next quote."

## Data shock

- "[N]% of homes in [region] have [problem] right now."
- "The average [X] in [region] is [$Y]. Here's how to pay [$Y - reduction]."
- "[year] data: [stat]."
- "[N] out of [M] [target] in [region] are overpaying for [thing]."
- "[N] grand. That's the average [thing] cost in [region] this year."

## Question

- "Why are [target] paying [$N] for [thing]?"
- "What if [thing] only took [time]?"
- "Would you let your [thing] go [N] more years without [action]?"
- "How much would it cost to fix [problem] today vs. next year?"

## Niche specifics

### Roofing

- "Most Texas roofs fail at year 12, not year 25."
- "[Region] hail season is here. Your roof has 8 weeks."
- "If you bought your home before [year], your insurance won't cover this."

### Remodeling

- "Your kitchen is dating your house."
- "[N] grand in [region] gets you twice what it did last year. Here's why."
- "Most contractors quote high, then ghost. Here's the test."

## What does NOT work

- "Welcome to [Company]!" → dead on arrival, scroll past.
- "Hi everyone, today I want to talk about..." → burns the first 4s.
- "Are you tired of [problem]?" → too generic, parodied to death.
- "Transform your home today!" → banned word + zero specificity.
- "We are the premium, cutting-edge..." → banned words, no human reads past it.
