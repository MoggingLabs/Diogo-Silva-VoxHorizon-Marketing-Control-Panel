## Context

What is the situation that forces a decision? Describe the problem, the
forces at play, and the constraints. Keep it factual: what is true today,
what is broken, what we are trying to protect. Link to the rebuild plan,
issues, or prior ADRs where relevant.

## Decision

What we decided to do, stated in the active voice ("We will ..."). Be
specific enough that a reader can tell whether a future change conforms to
or violates this decision. Note the single source of truth this decision
establishes, if any.

## Consequences

What becomes easier and what becomes harder as a result. Include the
positive outcomes, the new obligations (drift gates, codegen, review
ownership), and any accepted trade-offs or residual risk. List follow-on
work this decision implies.
