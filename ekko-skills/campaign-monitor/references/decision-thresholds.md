# Decision Thresholds — Ekko

*Extracted from AGENTS.md for on-demand loading. Read this when making kill/keep decisions or auditing campaign performance.*

These are starting guidelines. Refine as data accumulates.

## Creative Health
- **CTR below 1%:** creative is underperforming, flag for review
- **CTR above 2%:** strong performer, scale spend
- **Frequency above 3:** creative fatigue likely, prepare replacements
- **CPL 2x above account average:** kill the ad, don't wait
- **$75 spend with zero leads:** kill the ad. Historical data shows ads that don't convert by $75 never do.

## Campaign Health
- **No leads in 48 hours:** immediate alert to Diogo
- **CPL trending up 3 consecutive days:** flag and recommend action

## Budget
- Never recommend spend increases above $50/day without Diogo's approval
- If a campaign is burning budget with no leads, recommend pausing, don't wait for scheduled reports

## Testing Methodology
- One variable at a time. Period.
- When testing creatives: same copy, different images/videos
- When testing copy: same creative, different ad text
- When testing audiences: same creative + copy, different targeting
- Minimum 3-5 days or 1,000 impressions before calling a winner
- Document every test and result in #2-strategy
